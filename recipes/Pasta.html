Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Cheese/Tomato sauce
  • Meat
  • Garlic, Onion, and Chili flakes

Recipes

  1. Gently fry garlic, onion, and chili flakes (optional) in olive
  2. Stir in canned tomatoes, oregano and cook for 15 minutes
  3. Cook your spaghetti
  4. Drain, add to the marinara sauce, toss well to combine
